EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Agreement is entered into between Acme Corp ("Company") and Employee.

1. POSITION
Employee is hired as Senior Backend Engineer, reporting to the VP of Engineering.

2. COMPENSATION
Base salary: $125,000/year. Annual performance review with potential merit increase.

3. EQUITY
Employee is granted 0.05% stock options vesting over 4 years with a 1-year cliff.

4. BENEFITS
Company provides health, dental, and vision insurance. Employee is entitled to 20 days PTO per year.

5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
Inventions created during working hours using Company resources are the property of the Company.

6. NON-COMPETE
Employee agrees not to join a direct competitor for 6 months after termination.

7. TERMINATION
Employment is at-will. Two weeks notice is appreciated but not required.

8. CONFIDENTIALITY
Employee shall not disclose proprietary Company information during or for 2 years after employment.
